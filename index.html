About NeoMotion

Modern physiotherapy built around real movement.

NeoMotion PhysioTherapy is a clinic based in Indrapuram focused on helping people recover from pain, injury, and surgery with practical treatment that supports long-term results.

We combine hands-on care, movement analysis, and progressive exercise therapy to create treatment plans that fit your goals, schedule, and activity level.

What makes us different

  • ✓ Evidence-based treatment strategies grounded in current rehabilitation practice.
  • ✓ Personalized plans designed around work demands, sport, and lifestyle.
  • ✓ Strong focus on sports rehabilitation and return-to-performance pathways.
  • ✓ Clear education so patients understand the cause of pain and the recovery plan.
  • ✓ Goal tracking and home programs that keep progress moving between visits.
