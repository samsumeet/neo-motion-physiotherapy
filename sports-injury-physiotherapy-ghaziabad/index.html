Sports injuries we commonly treat

What sports rehab includes

Frequently asked questions

Do you treat sports injuries in Ghaziabad?

Yes. NeoMotion helps with sprains, strains, overload pain, gym injuries, and sports-related recovery planning.

Can physiotherapy help me return to sport safely?

Yes. Rehab can improve pain, movement quality, strength, and confidence before you return to sport or training.

Do I need to stop all activity completely?

Not always. Many injuries improve with smart activity modification instead of total rest, depending on the case.

Can sports rehab help gym-related pain too?

Yes. Sports injury physiotherapy can also help with gym-related pain, lifting strain, and repeated training overload.
